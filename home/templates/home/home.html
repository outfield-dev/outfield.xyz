Mapping for Precision Farming

Outfield aerial mapping forms an essential input to your precision farming system. Using lightweight multi-spectral cameras, fields are quickly and accurately mapped from the air. Plots of vegetation index (either NDVI or SARI) are generated, indicating levels of plant stress across the whole field. This data can be easily related to crop nitrogen levels, allowing you to precisely target your fertiliser applications.

Precision farming in this manner can help you achieve more uniform yields across your field, increase total yields, reduce your input costs, and minimise your environmental impact.

Contact us at the bottom of this page to find out more about Outfield precision farming.

Field Margin Mapping

If you maintain field margins as part of a Countryside Stewardship Scheme, you will be keen to ensure those margins are the correct size to secure your payments and avoid hefty fines. Using our aerial mapping systems, Outfield can quickly and accurately survey field margins to ensure they meet the required width.

Whether you are self-auditing, or just for piece of mind, Outfield can survey many kilometres of field boundaries in a fraction of the time of a full RPA inspection. Areas where corrective measures should be taken are highlighted, and you can easily review back-to-back comparisons with previous surveys.

Contact us at the bottom of this page to find out more about Outfield field margin mapping.

About Outfield

At Outfield we are passionate about unmanned aerial systems (UASs or drones). We envision a world where flying robots help us with daily tasks; from agriculture to search and rescue, from industrial inspection to traffic management. We are striving to build systems for drones to interact meaningfully with the world around them, while operating safely and responsibly. We operate both multi-rotor and fixed wing aircraft.
