What is the future of agriculture?

We believe the future lies in quick and simple aerial surveys providing data to drive better farming practices. Outfield is building systems that improve efficiency and save farmers time and money.

Designed by growers, built by us.

How does it work?

Our approach is focussed on spending time face to face with farmers, in order to develop effective solutions to current problems. Combining local knowledge of farming with Outfield's expertise in drone operation and imagery analysis, we are developing new systems that will help your farm, today.

Our current projects include:

Field Margin Mapping

Fast and accurate aerial surveys of field boundaries to help you meet your Countryside Stewardship Scheme obligations

Precision Farming

Detailed scans of vegetation index and biomass data, that plug straight into your current precision farming systems

Weed Detection

High resolution plots highlighting problem areas, providing you with the tools you needs for better weed management

Prioritising Safety and Privacy

Our highest priorities are public safety and the privacy of our clients. All of our operations are fully compliant with UK drone legislation.

What happens next?

Outfield is working with farmers and researchers to develop a range of agricultural survey solutions. We’re always interested in discussing problems and possible solutions, and in forming new partnerships with industry.

If you’re interested in any of the information here, would like to be involved, or would like to discuss our services further, get in contact with us at the bottom of this page.
