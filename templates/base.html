What is the future of agriculture?

We believe the future lies in quick and simple aerial surveys providing data to drive better farming practices. Outfield is building systems that improve efficiency and save farmers time and money. Designed by growers, built by us.

How does it work?

Our approach is focussed on spending time face to face with farmers, in order to develop effective solutions to current problems. Combining local knowledge of farming with Outfield's expertise in drone operation and imagery analysis, we are developing new systems that will help your farm, today. Our current projects include:

Field Margin Mapping

Fast and accurate aerial surveys of field boundaries to help you meet your Countryside Stewardship Scheme obligations

Precision Farming Inputs

Detailed scans of vegetation index and biomass data, that plug straight into your current precision farming systems

Weed Detection

High resolution plots highlighting problem areas, providing you with the tools you needs for better weed management
